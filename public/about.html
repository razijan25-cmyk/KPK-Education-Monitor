Our Mission

Monitoring and Advancing KPK Education

KPEdu is a research-driven platform dedicated to identifying systemic discrepancies in the provincial education system and proposing evidence-based solutions.

Our focus is on long-term infrastructure readiness, human capital development, and policy reform to ensure a digitally sovereign Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

Editorial Leadership

Junaid Khan serves as the Editor in Chief, overseeing the strategic analysis of curriculum shifts and pedagogical evolution within the province.
